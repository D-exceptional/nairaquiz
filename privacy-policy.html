Privacy Policy For NairaQuiz

We value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy informs our users on how we collect, use, store, and disclose their personal information when they visit our website. By accessing our website, you agree to the following terms and conditions of our Privacy Policy.



Information We Collect


Personal Information

We may request some personal information when you purchase or create an account on our website. Such information may include your name, email address, phone number, and payment information.



Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain information about your visit to our website, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and the referring website.



How We Use Your Information

Collected information is used in the following ways:

- Personalize your experience on our website.

- Provide adequate customer service experience.

- Update you on our products and services (orders, promotional offers, and newsletters). You may unsubscribe from these communications at any time.

- Better our services and improve our website functionality

- Utilize secure payment processors to handle your payment information. Note that we do not store or have access to your complete payment details such as credit card numbers, as such information is managed by the payment processor.



Information Sharing

Legal Obligations

We may disclose your personal information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.



Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your personal information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, processing payments, delivering orders, or analyzing website performance. These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and may only use it for the purposes specified by us.



Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience and collect information about how you use our website. You may allow or reject cookies when visiting our website. You may also disable cookies through your browser settings.



Data Security

We protect your personal information with industry-standard security measures from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.



Children's Privacy

Children under the age of 16 are not to use our website. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 16. Parents or guardians who believe that their child has provided us with personal information can contact us to remove that information from our systems.



Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will inform our users of changes to our Privacy Policy and make it available on our website. If you have any inquiries, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, kindly contact us. By using our website, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection, use, storage, and disclosure of your personal information as described in the terms and conditions of the Privacy Policy.
